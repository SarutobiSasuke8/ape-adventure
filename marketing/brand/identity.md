# Brand Identity

## One-liner

**Ape Adventure** — a 90s-style jungle platformer where one ape takes on a Saurian invasion.

## Tagline

> **Run. Roll. Rescue.**

Three verbs. One adventure. Use it everywhere — title art, store header, social bio, end of trailer.

## Mission

Capture the **feel** of a 1990s SNES-era jungle platformer — the momentum, the secrets, the music you remember in the shower years later — built fresh in 2026 with original characters, original IP, and a polished tile-and-sprite craft.

We're not making a fan game. We're making the kind of game that *made us love games in the first place.*

## Brand pillars

1. **Momentum is the music.** Every system serves flow. Run, roll, swing, pounce. Stopping is a choice, never a punishment.
2. **The jungle is alive.** Vines sway, water shimmers, birds startle. The world is a character.
3. **Secrets reward curiosity.** Bonus barrels behind waterfalls. Hidden paths in tree hollows. Exploration is rewarded but never required.
4. **Soundtrack-first.** A 90s platformer lives or dies on its music. We commission real tracks and treat them like the lead instrument.
5. **Original IP, made by hand.** Inspired by the genre, never imitating any one game's marks. Every character, level, and tune is ours.

## What we are not

- A *Donkey Kong Country* fan game or clone.
- A pixel-art retro throwback.
- A modern roguelite or metroidvania.
- A mobile free-to-play.
- An NFT, Web3, or "play-to-earn" anything.

## Personality

If *Ape Adventure* were a person, they'd be: a 90s kid all grown up, who still has their SNES under the TV, who notices when someone gets the music *right*. Warm. Curious. Slightly nostalgic but not sentimental. Loves a well-hidden secret.

- Tag-team second character — *door open* for full game
- Vehicles (mine cart, barrel cannon) — *door open*
- Swimming — *door open*
- Multiple worlds beyond Tangle Jungle — *door open*
- Story cutscenes — environmental storytelling only
