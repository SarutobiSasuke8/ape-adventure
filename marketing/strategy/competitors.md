# Competitive Landscape

We don't compete on "best 2D platformer" — that's a crowded shelf. We compete on a **specific frame**: *modern, original-IP, painted 90s-console platformer with a soundtrack you'd listen to outside the game.*

## Direct neighbors (similar genre / vibe)

| Title | What they do | What we do differently |
|---|---|---|
| *Donkey Kong Country* (1994) trilogy | The genre touchstone we admire | We're original IP, never a fan game; modern build, fresh worlds |
| *Yoshi's Island* (1995) | Painterly look, generous platforming | Different cast, different scope, no Mario |
| *Rayman Legends* (2013) | Modern lush 2D platformer | More chill / less frantic; smaller scope |
| *Hollow Knight* (2017) | Atmosphere + depth + original IP | We're momentum-first, not metroidvania |
| *Pizza Tower* (2023) | Frantic, cartoon, indie hit | We're warmer / mellower, not punk-cartoon |
| *Ori and the Blind Forest* (2015) | Painted, atmospheric, melancholy | We're warm and sunny, not melancholic |

## What competitors do *better* (be honest)

- ***DKC* trilogy:** 30 years of brand recognition. Iconic music. Nintendo distribution.
- ***Hollow Knight*:** Massive scope, perfect soundtrack, moody depth.
- ***Celeste*:** Tightest controls in the genre, strong narrative spine.
- ***Pizza Tower*:** Distinctive style, strong meme energy.
- ***Ori*:** Painterly visuals at AAA budget.

## What competitors do *worse* (where we win)

- **Most pixel-art jam platformers:** Often unfinished or thin. Ours will ship.
- **Pixel-art "DKC remakes":** Legally fraught. Ours is original IP.
- **Modern AAA platformers:** Charge $30+. Ours is free / pay-what-you-want for the slice.
- **Pure nostalgia projects:** Cosplay the look. We chase the feel.

## Lessons we'll borrow

- ***DKC*** taught us: **the music is the brand**. Commission tracks before you commission art.
- ***Yoshi's Island*** taught us: **painterly + warm + generous = comforting**. Don't be afraid to be sweet.
- ***Hollow Knight*** taught us: **atmosphere is built in layers** — parallax, music, particles, light, all working together.
- ***Celeste*** taught us: **a tight 6-second clip outperforms any trailer**. Capture the moment, ship the moment.
- ***Pizza Tower*** taught us: **a strong distinctive style cuts through a crowded shelf**. Don't be generic.

## Lessons we'll *avoid*

- ***Donkey Kong*** clones taught us: **the legal grey is not worth it**. Original IP, always.
- **Some pixel jams** taught us: **ship the polish, not the prototype**. We don't release the slice until it feels good.
